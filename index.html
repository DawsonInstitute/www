About the Institute

The Dawson Institute for Advanced Physics is an unincorporated research group dedicated to exploring the fundamental aspects of physics, with particular focus on spacetime manipulation, quantum gravity, and advanced energy systems.

Our research combines rigorous theoretical frameworks with innovative computational approaches to address some of the most challenging questions in modern physics.
